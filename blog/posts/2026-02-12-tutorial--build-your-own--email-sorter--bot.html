The Stack (Simplified)

Here's what you'll need (don't worry, it's easier than it sounds):

  • Python: Think of this as the engine. It's the backbone of our bot, but we'll keep it super simple.
  • Gemini (or your preferred AI model): This is the brain. It analyzes the email content and decides where it goes.
  • A Gmail account: Where your emails live.

We're not going to get bogged down in technical jargon. I'll walk you through each step, holding your hand. Seriously. It's easier than setting up your Facebook profile.

Auto-Sorting Magic

Here's the concept:

  1. Our Python "engine" connects to your Gmail.
  2. It pulls in new emails.
  3. It sends the email content to Gemini (the AI brain).
  4. Gemini analyzes the email and tells us what it's about (e.g., "Sales Lead," "Customer Support," "Spam").
  5. Our Python engine then automatically moves the email to the correct folder.

Boom. Sorted. Automatically.
